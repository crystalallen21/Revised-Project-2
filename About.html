Ariana Grande is a 27 year old American singer and actress. Her career started at 15 years old by being in Broadway and attracting the attention from directors and producers for her four-octave vocal range and whistle register. She then landed a role as Cat from TV show Victorious followed by her own spin off show Sam and Cat. Later she made it big in the music industry, releasing 5 albums that include award winning songs.
